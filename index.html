VISHAL & SHEKHAR

India's biggest musical duo

Vishal-Shekhar, formed in 1999, is an Indian music producing duo comprising of prominent musicians Vishal Dadlani and Shekhar Ravjiani. They regularly compose music for Hindi films, many of which have gone on to be blockbuster hits.
